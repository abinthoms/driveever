## 📊 **User Behavior Patterns**

### **Primary User Types:**

1. **Vehicle Checkers (40%)**
   - Direct vehicle check users
   - No registration required
   - High engagement, low conversion

2. **Instructor Seekers (35%)**
   - Traditional driving lesson users
   - High conversion to registration
   - Long-term platform users

3. **Mixed Users (25%)**
   - Use both services
   - Highest lifetime value
   - Most likely to refer others
